  1. Onigiri rice ball
      2. Tips
    • use plastic wrap to shape rice ball
    • sprinkle salt on the wrap

Ingredients:

  • 150g rice
  • 1 tbsp tuna
  • 1/2 tbsp mayo
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 piece of seaweed

Instructions:

  1. open 1 canned tuna
  2. drain the oil or water
  3. take 1/2 tbsp tuna from the canned tuna
  4. take 1/2 tbsp mayo
  5. mix well and then the filling is ready!
  6. use plastic wrap to shape onigiri because bacteria can spread if you don't
  7. Sprinkle salt on the wrap
  8. put the rice on that plastic wrap
  9. dig a little hole for the filing
  10. try to put the filling inside
  11. make a triangle (you can also use the Riceball mould to make the shape)
  12. 1 dried seaweed
